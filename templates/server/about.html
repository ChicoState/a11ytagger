About a11ytagger

Our Mission

a11ytagger is a Django-based web application designed to make PDF documents more accessible to everyone. We believe that digital content should be accessible to all users, regardless of their abilities or the assistive technologies they use.

Our tool helps users add proper accessibility tags to PDF documents, ensuring that screen readers and other assistive technologies can properly interpret and present the content. By automating and simplifying the accessibility tagging process, we're making it easier for content creators to produce inclusive, accessible documents.

What We Do

a11ytagger provides an intuitive interface for:

  • Uploading and analyzing PDF documents
  • Automatically detecting accessibility issues
  • Adding proper structure tags to PDF content
  • Ensuring compliance with accessibility standards
  • Generating accessible PDFs that work seamlessly with assistive technologies

The Team

a11ytagger is developed and maintained by a dedicated team of students and faculty at California State University, Chico. Our team combines expertise in software development, accessibility standards, and user experience design to create a tool that makes a real difference in digital accessibility.

We're committed to continuous improvement and welcome feedback from our users. This project is open source, and we encourage contributions from the community to help make the web more accessible for everyone.
